Why is this query returning null for the joined column?
The issue is a LEFT JOIN with a mismatched condition. Your ON clause references user_id but the right table uses account_id.
sql 
SELECT u.name, o.total
FROM users u
LEFT JOIN orders o
  ON u.id = o.account_id
Change user_id to account_id and it should resolve.
