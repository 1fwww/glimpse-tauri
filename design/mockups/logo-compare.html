What changed
1.Eyebrow centered over eye. Original eyebrow center was 12px right of the eye center. Now perfectly aligned at x=259.
2.Eyebrow tightened (moderately). ~25px closer to the eye — splits the difference between the original gap (73px) and the too-tight version. Brow reads as connected to the eye but still has room to breathe and wiggle.
3.Eyebrow ends balanced. Original: start y=204, end y=251 (47px drop). Now: start y=220, end y=256 (36px drop). Still has the natural slight droop right, but less extreme.
4.Bottom-right spiral join smoothed. Tangent discontinuity reduced from 8.8° to near 0° (collinear control points). No visible kink.
5.Clean integer coordinates. All pen-tool decimals (104.539, 374.28, etc.) rounded to integers. Cleaner SVG, identical rendering.
6.Wiggle transform-origin updated to match new eyebrow center (260px, 220px).
7.Same viewBox, stroke widths, color, proportions. Drop-in replacement — same `viewBox="60 140 420 280"`, same `stroke-width="20"` / `"22"`.
