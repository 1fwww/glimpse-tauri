The panel lifts, gains a brand accent bar, thicker border, deeper shadow, slight scale, and backdrop blur. These are all perceivable without color vision.
What changes on pin
Shadow lift: flat → deep layered shadow with brand tint. Panel feels elevated.
Border: 1px neutral → 1.5px brand-tinted. Thicker and colored.
Scale: 1.0 → 1.015. Subtle but creates a "came toward you" feeling.
Backdrop blur: 20px blur + saturation boost. Content behind becomes frosted.
Pin button: dim → brand-filled. Both color AND fill density change.
Surface tint: warm gray → soft lavender. Layered on top, not the primary signal.
Accessibility: Shadow, scale, accent bar, border weight, and blur are all perceivable without color vision. Color tint is additive, not the sole signal.
